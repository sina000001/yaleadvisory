Yale Advisory Group advises the most ambitious and consequential organizations in the world on strategy, technical analysis, market intelligence, and the adoption of emerging technology. The firm's core principles are rigor, discretion, selectivity, and an uncompromising standard of output. Yale Advisory Group is composed of a small, carefully chosen cohort of analysts, all Yale undergraduates selected by invitation, each holding offers at the world's foremost institutions and firms.

Yale Advisory Group was founded on the conviction that the most rigorous analytical talent in the world should not be confined to institutions and that organizations facing consequential decisions deserve access to it. The firm takes a small number of engagements each semester, staffed entirely by analysts chosen for fit rather than availability, and holds every deliverable to an institutional standard.
